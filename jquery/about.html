About Prosares

Prosares is an information technology company offering specialist enterprise solutions and services. Since its inception in 2002, the company has created value for customers by nurturing deep expertise in specific technology domains. The company enjoys long-standing relationship with several large enterprise customers in India, Middle East and USA.​​ Prosares’ growth plans are focussed around expanding the footprint of its solutions and services by creating last mile delivery capability and by forging alliances in global markets.
